Science Driven Optimization of LSST's Observing Strategy

A community workshop on the optimization of LSST observing strategy, leading to a white paper quantified via the the Metric Analysis Framework.

Welcome

We are planning a LSST science collaborations workshop on "Science Driven Optimization of LSST's Observing Strategy" in summer 2015. More details will appear here in time; for now, you can follow along with the plans being made here.

People

The scientific organizing committee for this workshop consists of Zeljko Ivezic (chair), Debbie Bard, Andy Connolly, Phil Marshall, Tom Matheson, Steve Ridgeway, Lucianne Walkowicz, and Beth Willman.

This page is being developed on the LSST Science Collaborations GitHub by Zeljko Ivezic, Phil Marshall and others.
